## 👋 About Me

I'm a **Backend Engineer** specializing in **Geospatial Systems** — passionate about designing efficient, scalable, and reliable back-end solutions that connect data with location.

I bridge the gap between traditional software engineering and spatial intelligence, building systems that don't just store data — they **make it meaningful**.

- 🔭 I work on **Geospatial APIs**, spatial data pipelines, and location-aware services
- 🧱 I build with **Django REST Framework**, **FastAPI**, **ASP.NET Core**, and **PostGIS**
- 🗺️ I process and analyze spatial data using **GDAL**, **GeoPandas**, **ArcPy**, and **QGIS**
- 📚 Currently pursuing an **academic diploma in Computer Science**
- 💬 I enjoy solving complex technical challenges that have **real-world impact**
- 🤝 Open to collaboration on geospatial, backend, or data-driven projects
